Related Research

The usage of technologies in cultural heritage contexts has being an interesting subject to explore and a vast amount of interesting research studies are found. This page presents the related research created by the main author of this website (Anabela Marto), regarding to SensiMAR project and, also, to its previous version - the DinofelisAR, a mobile augmented reality application tested in the Roman ruins of Conimbriga in 2017.
