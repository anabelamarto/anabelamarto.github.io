Related Research

The usage of technologies in cultural heritage contexts has being an interesting subject to explore and a vast amount of interesting research studies are found. This page presents the related research created by the author of this website (Anabela Marto), specifically regarding to SensiMAR or DinofilisAR studies. To access the full research carried out by the author, please acess to her personal page, or her ResearchGate page or her OrcID page .
