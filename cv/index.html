About Me
33 years old · Portuguese · +351 916 817 894 · anabellamarto@gmail.com

I am experienced in exploring solutions for AR applications since 2015, currently working on a multisensory AR approach for cultural heritage. I'm also a lecturer at Polytechnic Institute of Leiria for 6 years, and a researcher at CIIC (ESTG-IPLeiria), and at INESC TEC, Porto.

In short, my main skills are related to 3D production (Blender, Maya, 3D Studio Max), AR app development (Unity, AR SDks such as Vuforia or AR Core), Graphics (Photoshop, Illustrator), Video Editing (After Effects, Premiere, DaVinci Resolve), Web development (HTML5, Hugo), and Animation 2D (Animator, Spriter 2D.)
