About SensiMAR

SensiMAR is the given name to a multisensory project created in the scope of a PhD in Informatics, in University of Trás-os-Montes and Alto Douro, Vila Real (Portugal). Sensi was inspired in the word «senses» and MAR stands for «Multisensory Augmented Reality».

This project includes the following tasks:

  • Literature Review
  • Technology Acceptance Study
  • Multisensorial AR Prototype
  • Collection of Results
  • Discussion of Results

Want to know more?

This study is being carried out by the PhD student Anabela Marto, with supervision of Professor Maximino Bessa (University of Trás-os-Monted and Alto Douro, Portugal) and Professor Alexandrino Gonçalves (Polythecnic Institute of Leiria, Portugal).

Short bio (Anabela Marto): Anabela Marto is an Invited Lecturer in the Department of Informatics Engineering at the Superior School of Technology and Management (ESTG) - Polytechnic of Leiria (IPLeiria) - and a Researcher at Computer Science and Communication Research Centre (CIIC). Her research topics are related to Mobile Augmented Reality applied in Cultural Heritage contexts. She is graduated in Computer Engineering (ESTG - IPLeiria), has a master degree in Multimedia - specialization in Technologies (FEUP - Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto) and she is a PhD student in Informatics (UTAD - University of Trás-os-Montes and Alto Douro).
