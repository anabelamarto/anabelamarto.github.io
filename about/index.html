About SensiMAR

SensiMAR is the given name to a multisensory study in the scope of a PhD in Informatics, at University of Trás-os-Montes and Alto Douro, Vila Real (Portugal). Sensi- was inspired in the word «senses» and -MAR stands for «Multisensory Augmented Reality».

This project includes the following main tasks:

  • Literature Review
  • Technology Acceptance Study
  • Multisensorial AR Prototype
  • Collection of Results
  • Discussion of Results

About this Website

This website provides pieces of information related to SensiMAR subject area including, inter alia, posts related to Cultural Heritage Sites and related to Technologies - mainly, Augmented Reality and Multisensory approaches.
