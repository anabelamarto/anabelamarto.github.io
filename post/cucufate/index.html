About Anabela Marto
Anabela Marto is an Invited Lecturer in the Computer Engineering Department at the Superior School of Technology and Management (ESTG) - Polytechnic of Leiria (IPLeiria) - and a Researcher at Computer Science and Communication Research Centre (CIIC). Her research topics are related to Mobile Augmented Reality applied in Cultural Heritage contexts. She is graduated in Computer Engineering (ESTG - IPLeiria), has a master degree in Multimedia - specialization in Technologies (FEUP - Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto) and she is conducting her PhD work in Informatics (UTAD - University of Trás-os-Montes and Alto Douro).
